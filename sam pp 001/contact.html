Frequently Asked Questions

How can I apply for a training?

Browse our courses section, select your preferred training program, and click on the "Apply Now" button. Follow the registration process to enroll.

Can companies post jobs here?

Yes! Companies can register on our platform and post job opportunities specifically targeted at rural talent. Visit our "For Companies" section to learn more.

How can I become a training partner?

Training partners can join us by filling out the partner application form. We'll review your credentials and get back to you with the next steps.

Is this platform free for rural students?

Yes, Rural Genius provides free access to basic training resources for rural students. Some advanced courses may have nominal fees.
